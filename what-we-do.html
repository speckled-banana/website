    Who 'We' Are

    Speckled Banana is essentially just me, Sean Thompson. I left my full time job in 2021 so that I could take care of some home improvements for my dad and scratch a bit of a travelling itch. I was approached during this time to do some freelance work and also wanted a vehicle for working on my own ideas and side projects, so one afternoon whilst staring at my lunch for inspiration Speckled Banana was formed!

    It's now one year on and I'm back in the UK and figuring I should start 'selling' myself a little hence finally getting around to setting up a website for the business.

    I've been workjing on building technical solutions to problems for a long while now, I set up my first website in 1999 at the age of 13, had various others throughout my teens and then started using my skills in a work capacity about 18 years ago.
