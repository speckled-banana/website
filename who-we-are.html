    Who Are Speckled Banana

    For larger projects I have a number of specialist associates that can work alongside me, however, most of the time Speckled Banana is just me, Sean Thompson.

    I left my full time consultancy job in 2021 so that I could take care of some family things and scratch a bit of a travelling itch. During this time I started to get asked about doing some freelance work.

    I'd already been thinking about setting up a company for working on my own ideas and side projects, so… one afternoon, whilst staring at my lunch for inspiration Speckled Banana was born!

    I love using technology to solve problems and I’ve been doing it for a long while now. I set up my first website in 1998 at the age of 12, had various others throughout my teens and then started using my skills in a work capacity 18 years ago.

    I’m a bit of a generalist and enjoy owning the end-to-end process, feeling just as comfortable with my head in some code as I am with my project manager hat on or providing training to a room of end-users.
