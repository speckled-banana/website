    Who we are and what we do

    Speckled Banana's team has over 15 years experience working on varied projects across a number of industries. We use that experience to help organisations that align with our values to deliver projects that are impactful for them and society.

    What we can help with

    • Software development
    • Database design and administration
    • Data analysis, dashboarding & business intelligence
    • Blog / website setup and hosting
    • Project scoping and requirements analysis
    • Training
    • Fixed term contracts
    • Project outsourcing
    • Digital strategy

    What we won't help with

    • Anything invading user privacy; google analytics, facebook pixel etc.
    • Implementation of 'dark' design patterns
    • Projects for businesses that operate in sectors having a detrimental impact on the planet, society or people's wellbeing
    • Crypto, day trading, get rich quick or anything else scammy

    Non Profit? We Love Helping Out Good Causes

    We have a desire to use our skills to do some good in the world. For that reason we can often offer pro bono / free of charge consultancy hours to non-profits and community projects that align with our values.
