    Speckled Banana Privacy Policy

    TL;DR: For speckledbanana.com visitors, we do not use cookies and we do not collect any personal data. If you decide to use the contact form, we only require your email address and perform no additional logging.

    At Speckled Banana, we are committed to complying with GDPR, CCPA, PECR and other privacy regulations on our website. The privacy of your data ‚Äî and it is your data, not ours! ‚Äî is a big deal to us.

    In this policy, we lay out what data we collect and why, how your data is handled and your rights to your data. We promise we never sell your data: never have, never will.

    As a visitor to the speckledbanana.com website

    The privacy of our website visitors is important to us so we do not track any individual people. As a visitor to the speckledbanana.com website:

    • No personal information is collected
    • No information such as cookies is stored in the browser
    • No information is shared with, sent to or sold to third-parties
    • No information is shared with advertising companies
    • No information is mined and harvested for personal and behavioral trends
    • No information is monetized
    • We self-host the Plausible Analytics script to collect some anonymous usage data for statistical purposes. The goal is to track overall trends in our website traffic, it is not to track individual visitors. All the data is in aggregate only. No personal data is collected.

    Data collected includes referral sources, top pages, visit duration, information from the devices (device type, operating system, country and browser) used during the visit and more. You can see full details in Plausible's data policy.

    As a user of our contact form

    We only ask that you provide us with your email address and this email address will only be used to reply to your message. It will not be added to a distribution list or used in future to contact you regarding other matters.

    For the form to work we need to send a request to a server operated by us but owned by Hetzner, a European company. On this server we do not log anything to do with your request but initiate sending an email to hello@speckledbanana.com using our email provider (currently Gmail).

    The only place that your email address is therefore stored is within our email inbox. Once we have dealt with your request and any follow up queries we will delete your correspondence.

    Changes and questions

    We may update this policy as needed to comply with relevant regulations and reflect any new practices.

    Contact us at hello@speckledbanana.com if you have any questions, comments, or concerns about this privacy policy, your data, or your rights with respect to your information.
